P-00.00-0004: HR-STD-006 Training & Development Standard

[SUBSECTION 00.01] 1. Purpose
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.01-1_PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.01-0009: Defines training eligibility, approval workflow, and certification reimbursement.

[SUBSECTION 00.02] 2. Annual Training Plan
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.02-2_ANNUAL_TRAINING_PLAN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.02-0014: Each employee must complete at least 2 trainings per year.
P-00.02-0015: Mandatory trainings: security, ethics, safety (if applicable).

[SUBSECTION 00.03] 3. Approvals
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.03-3_APPROVALS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.03-0020: Employee submits training request
P-00.03-0021: Line manager approves
P-00.03-0022: HR L&D validates budget
P-00.03-0023: Finance approval required if training exceeds SAR 7,500

[SUBSECTION 00.04] 4. Certification Reimbursement
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.04-4_CERTIFICATION_REIMBURSEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.04-0028: Only approved certifications are eligible.
P-00.04-0029: Employee must pass exam.
P-00.04-0030: Reimbursement up to SAR 5,000 per year.
