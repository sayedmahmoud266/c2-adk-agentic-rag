P-00.03-0029: 3) Final written warning

SECTION_ID: SEC-00.04-4_INVESTIGATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

P-00.04-0036: Investigation completed within 10 business days.
P-00.04-0037: Employee must be given chance to respond.

[SUBSECTION 00.05] 5. Appeals
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.05-5_APPEALS
