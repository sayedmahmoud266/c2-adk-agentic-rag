P-00.00-0004: HR-STD-003 Employee Onboarding & Probation Standard

[SUBSECTION 00.01] 1. Purpose
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.01-1_PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.01-0009: Ensures a consistent onboarding process and probation evaluation.

[SUBSECTION 00.02] 2. Scope
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.02-2_SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.02-0014: Applies to all newly hired employees and internal transfers.

[SUBSECTION 00.03] 3. Onboarding Activities (First 30 Days)
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.03-3_ONBOARDING_ACTIVITIES_FIRST_30_DAYS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.03-0019: Day 1 Requirements:
P-00.03-0020: Signed employment contract
P-00.03-0021: Employee ID creation
P-00.03-0022: IT access request submitted
P-00.03-0023: Workstation allocated
P-00.03-0024: Week 1:
P-00.03-0025: HR orientation session
P-00.03-0026: Code of Conduct acknowledgment
P-00.03-0027: Security awareness training
P-00.03-0028: Week 2- 4:
P-00.03-0029: Department orientation
P-00.03-0030: Assigned “Buddy”
P-00.03-0031: Manager onboarding plan (weekly check-ins)

[SUBSECTION 00.04] 4. Probation Period
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.04-4_PROBATION_PERIOD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.04-0036: Standard probation: 3 months
P-00.04-0037: Extended probation: up to 6 months (requires HR Director approval)

[SUBSECTION 00.05] 5. Probation Evaluation
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.05-5_PROBATION_EVALUATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.05-0042: Manager must submit evaluation after 60 days.
P-00.05-0043: Final evaluation by day 85.
P-00.05-0044: Outcomes: confirm employment, extend probation, or terminate employment (subject to regulations).
