P-00.00-0004: HR-STD-001 Attendance & Working Hours Standard

[SUBSECTION 00.01] 1. Purpose
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.01-1_PURPOSE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.01-0009: This standard defines working hours, attendance recording, breaks, overtime, and lateness rules.

[SUBSECTION 00.02] 2. Scope
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.02-2_SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.02-0014: Applies to all employees (permanent, contractual, and interns) working at the organization offices and remote work locations.

[SUBSECTION 00.03] 3. Working Hours
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.03-3_WORKING_HOURS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.03-0019: Standard working hours: 08:00 AM -  04:00 PM, Sunday to Thursday.
P-00.03-0020: Break: 1 hour break between 12:00 PM -  02:00 PM.
P-00.03-0021: Friday/Saturday are weekends unless business need requires work.

[SUBSECTION 00.04] 4. Attendance Recording
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.04-4_ATTENDANCE_RECORDING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.04-0026: Employees must record attendance using:
P-00.04-0027: HR Portal (web/mobile) OR
P-00.04-0028: Office biometric device (if applicable).

[SUBSECTION 00.05] 5. Lateness
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.05-5_LATENESS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.05-0033: Lateness is defined as arriving after 08:15 AM.
P-00.05-0034: Grace period: 15 minutes.
P-00.05-0035: Late arrival more than 3 times per month triggers an HR warning.

[SUBSECTION 00.06] 6. Absence
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.06-6_ABSENCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.06-0040: Unplanned absence must be reported to line manager before 09:00 AM.
P-00.06-0041: Absence without approval is considered Unauthorized Absence.

[SUBSECTION 00.07] 7. Overtime
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.07-7_OVERTIME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.07-0046: Overtime requires prior written approval from line manager.
P-00.07-0047: HR approval is required if overtime exceeds 10 hours per month.
P-00.07-0048: Overtime calculation:
P-00.07-0049: Weekdays: 1.25x hourly rate
P-00.07-0050: Weekends: 1.50x hourly rate

[SUBSECTION 00.08] 8. Exceptions
SECTION_ID: SEC-00.08-8_EXCEPTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
P-00.08-0055: Any exception to this standard must be approved by HR Director.
